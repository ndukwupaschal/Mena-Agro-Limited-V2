Why we exist

African agriculture doesn't lack potential. It lacks circular infrastructure.

Every year, millions of tonnes of organic waste rot in Nigerian markets while farmers pay punishing prices for imported feed and fertiliser. The materials of prosperity are being thrown away within walking distance of the people who need them.

MENA AGRO exists to close that gap — building the systems that capture waste, transform it biologically, and return it to farms as high-value inputs. Not charity. Not extraction. Infrastructure.
