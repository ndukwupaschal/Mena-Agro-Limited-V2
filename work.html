Our Work

Integrated solutions for resilient food systems.

One circular model, expressed through a growing portfolio of programmes — starting with Black Soldier Fly bioconversion, our first proof of concept.

The model

One loop. Five points of value.

I

Capture

Organic waste is collected from markets, farms, and processors before it becomes pollution.

II

Convert

Black Soldier Fly larvae biologically transform waste into protein-rich biomass in under 14 days.

III

Feed

Harvested larvae become premium protein for fish and poultry — replacing costly imported feed.

IV

Restore

Frass, the process residue, returns to fields as organic fertiliser that rebuilds living soil.

V

Regrow

Healthier farms produce more — and their byproducts re-enter the loop. Nothing is wasted.

Pilot cycle 01 — Owerri, 2026
First proof

The loop isn't a theory. We've already run it.

In 2026, our pilot in Owerri completed the full circle: organic waste in, protein feed and organic fertiliser out, tested live on fish and poultry. Small by design — cheap to start, fast to learn — and the proof of concept for everything we're building next.

A permanent production site has been secured at Umuguma, Owerri West. The next phase is scale.
